❓ How to Use

📥 Importing your city

  1. Install the FoE Helper browser extension.
  2. Open Forge of Empires and go to Town Overview → City Planner.
  3. Click "Copy citymap data" in FoE Helper.
  4. Click Import from FoE Helper in the sidebar, then paste.

🏗️ Placing buildings

  • Click a building in the list, then click on the canvas to place it.
  • Right-click a placed building to duplicate, stash, or delete it.
  • Drag a building off the canvas to send it to the Building Pool.

⚡ Optimizer

  • Automatically rearranges buildings to maximise road connectivity.
  • Use Undo Optimization to revert if you don't like the result.

🗺️ Tabs

  • Main City — your regular FoE city.
  • Settlement — cultural settlement (choose type in the sidebar).
  • Colony — space colony (choose type in the sidebar).
  • Quantum — Quantum Incursions city.

💾 Save & Share

  • Save exports a JSON you can paste back to reload your layout.
  • Share Layout generates a URL that encodes your entire layout — paste it anywhere to share.
  • PNG / PDF exports a snapshot of the canvas.
